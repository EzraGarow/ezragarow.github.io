About Me

I am a graduate student in History at Binghamton University, where I am working on my MA on the PhD track.

I am currently studying the Early American Jewish experience, with an eye towards investigating how Jewish culture and identity shaped individuals' responses to the American Revolution.

In my spare time, I also enjoy general religious history and the history of clothing and fashion.
